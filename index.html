It is a student-led society that strives to impart enthusiasm and passion for electronics and allied fields among the students. Samarthya believes in providing an environment that facilitates holistic development of the students.
Samarthya helped me enhance my confidence and develop my communication skills. Through this club, I learnt how to work in a 4-tier team. The innumerable discussions prior to organising various events definitely aided in changing my perspective on where to start while tackling a problem, this change played a role in boosting my problem solving skills.

The whole process of creating a design from scratch then watching it come together piece by piece is powerfully exhilarating.
